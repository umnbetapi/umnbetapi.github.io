Parent's Day

Beta loves to keep parents involved and we hold a Parent's Day in the spring to help showcase what we are all about. If you want more information about Parent's Day and how to become more involved with Beta's Parents Club, contact the current Paren'ts Club Liaison or Programming Chair.

Homecoming

Homecoming is a great time when the greek community leads the campus in showing school spirit Here is a great picture of Beta Pi's pomping effort at Homecoming in 2016.

Sheridan Story

Here betas helped pack food for local children who don't get enough to eat at home. We learned more about how hunger affects people everywhere, even in first world countries. Packing food was a great time made even better knowing we were helping children around the metro.

Intramurals

Beta's broomball team is a force to be reckoned with. This year we finished second in the greek league. We also have teams in football and next year are looking to get a soccer team. Go Beta!

Cystic Fibrosis Foundation

Beta is proud to sponsor CFF in efforts to help improve the daily lives of people with cystic fibrosis. Beta has had multiple philanthropy events like Light the Row and Beta Theta Pies to help raise money and awareness for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. Beta Pi also helps out annually by volunteering at the CFF Tower Climb and the CFF Breath of Life Gala.

Brotherhood Weekend

IT'S FINALLY HERE! Betas love Brotherhood Weekend. It's a great time to get to know all of your brothers as well as play in the classic Beta football game, adventure, and play mafia. Best weekend of the year!

Beta Theta Pies

Before going away for the summer, we have one last philanthropy every year that is always a smashing success. People come to enjoy some great pie, or perhaps throw a pie in a Beta's face. The philanthropy helped raise money for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation and the Aurora Center at the U.

Light the Row

Every winter, Beta hosts Light The Row which is our philanthropy for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. We light up the house and sync it up with some music. People come by to decorate cookies, socialize, and admire the show.

Formal

Formal is a great time to get to know your brothers and relax for a weekend in a grueling college semester. Here the Fall of 2016 pledge class poses for a quick pic before dinner.

Professor's Dinner

Every year we hold a professor's dinner where every Beta is encouraged to invite a professor you really like, or would like to get to know better. It is a great place to gain stronger connections with your professors and get to know them on a personal level.

Relay For Life

Beta is proud to always having an outstanding showing at Relay for Life. Relay For Life is a fundraiser for teh American Cancer Society. Relay also helps unite cancer survivors and people on campus through fun events.

Exchanges

Exchanges are great opportunities to get to meet more members of the greek community. We have several per semester and are always looking for new fun ideas. Here is a picture of our yoga exchange this year.
